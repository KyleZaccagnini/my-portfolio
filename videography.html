VIDEOGRAPHY

This is the section for all of my work in videography. I am a high interest in videography and video editing specifically. Most of these projects consist of videos I made for my student broadcast that I led as well as videos from my film and video editing class I took at the University of Connecticut.

Watertown High School Mascot Reveal

My senior year of high school I was asked by one of the staff of the high school to create a video that would reveal our new mascot as we had changed to the warriors. They got an alum who is now a voice actor to speak in the video and I edited it all myself in Adobe Premiere Pro.

Tame Impala Concert Video

After attending my favorite artist Tame Impala in concert I was inspired to make a video with the clips that I took on my phone at the concert. I wanted to encapsulate his vibe as an artist along with how the music makes me feel and think. I used Adobe After Effects to create all of this. The song Feels Like We Only Go Backwards by Tame Impala is used for only Portfolio Use.

Darling I, Music Video

In my Film and Video Editing 1 class I was tasked for my final project to make a music video of a song of our choice that had a cohesive storyline. I decided to use a song from Tyler the Creator and made a story about a man who is deciding between dating someone or remaining as someone who isn't locked down to anyone. I acted in the video, as well as edited the entire video. The use of the song Darling I, by Tyler the Creator is for Portfolio use only.

Watertown High School Broadcast Open

In my senior year of high school I acted as the student leader of our student broadcast and was tasked with making the open to our weekly broadcast. All videos that are in this video I had taken and then edited myself. I used Adobe Premiere Pro to do all of this and loved getting experience working with a team that I could lead.

Watertown High School Broadcast End of Year Open

In my senior year of high school I was tasked with making the final opening for our student led broadcast and at the time I was experimenting with Adobe After Effects and 3d elements so I made a 3d book that would flip through different memories of the broadcast over the year. Using Adobe After Effects was very new for me at the time and I was very experimental with things as I wanted to learn more about it and grow!
