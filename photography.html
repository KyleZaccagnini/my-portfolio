ABOUT ME

PHOTOGRAPHY

This section of my portfolio highlights my work in photography, showcasing the moments, environments, and perspectives I’ve captured through the lens. Each image represents my creative approach to composition, lighting, and storytelling. Whether it’s portraits, landscapes, or candid scenes, this collection reflects my style, growth, and passion for visual expression.

Track Series

I ran track all 4 years of high school and for my senior year I took it upon myself to take pictures of my teammates running so they would have photos of themselves for future endeavours. Many of my teammates would go on to using these for their commitment posts to run collegiately. I would run around between my events to take photos and was glad when I got a good shot that my teammates were excited to post!

Swim Series

All throughout High School I swam for my high school swim team and we did not have very much funding so we never were able to get actions photos of us swimming so one meet I took it upon myself to take photos for everyone between my events. I like how some of these shots came out and was happy that my teammates would be able to have photos of themselves swimming.

Eye Series

For my design foundations 2 course we were asked to use two skills that we know to create an image. I chose to use my photography and photo editing skills to create images where there are different natural disasters happening inside of my eye. This is supposed to imitate someone looking at a disaster like this. I used Adobe Photoshop to complete this assignment.

Professional Photos

My mother works for the Connecticut State Department of Pardons and Paroles and I used my photography skills to take professional headshots for her that she can use for her professional pages. She currently uses these photos for her LinkedIn as well as on the State website. We chose to take them in front of foliage to create a more inviting picture.
